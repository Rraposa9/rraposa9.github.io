Self portrait painting

2022

My painted self portrait was one of the last paintings completed in my painting class. We had to paint a self portrait that focused on the principles of color, form, and space. This piece taught me a lot about how to match colors based on just looking at them, and placing them on a canvas to create a believable portrait. We also got to incorporate reflections into this painting, which helped create a further sense of space.
