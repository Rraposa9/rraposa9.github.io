Sequence Comic

2022

This simple comic was made in one of my illustration classes where we had to make a sequence depicting a simple action. We had to have six unique panels and a simple action; I chose someone looking into the mirror. I used a brush pen for the larger filled areas, and a micron for inking. This project also made us simplify characters, locations, and objects to still be recognizable but simple for an easy reading experience.
