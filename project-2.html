Skate Painting

2022

The Ice Skate Painting was done in my painting course to highlight color temperature. We set up a still-life in a box with white objects and had a warm stable light. From there we were able to identify the warm lighting and cool shadows that the objects cast. This painting introduced me to the important qualities that color temperature has and how it affects how colors look. By having just white objects, some of them leaned warmer when lit and cooler when in shadow.
